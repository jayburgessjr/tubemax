Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2025

TubeMax ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the TubeMax application.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we request access to the following information:

  • Google profile information — your name, email address, and profile picture, used to display your account within the app.
  • YouTube data — your subscriptions, playlists, and liked videos, used to personalize your TubeMax experience.

We also store the following data locally in your browser using localStorage:

  • Watch history and continue-watching progress
  • App settings (theme preference, API key)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the TubeMax application:

  • To display your YouTube subscriptions, playlists, and personalized content
  • To remember your viewing progress and preferences
  • To authenticate you via Google OAuth 2.0

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Google API Services

TubeMax uses the YouTube Data API v3 and Google OAuth 2.0. Your use of TubeMax is therefore also subject to Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

TubeMax's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data Storage and Security

TubeMax is a client-side application. Your data is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers. We do not operate any backend database that stores your personal information.

Your Google OAuth access token is held in browser memory for the duration of your session and is not persisted to disk.

5. Data Retention

Local data (watch history, settings) persists in your browser until you clear it via the app's Settings page or clear your browser's local storage. You can remove all locally stored data at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

TubeMax is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
